Converse Preorder Inorder Postorder

If the words left and right are interchanged in the definitions of the traversal orders, the new traversal orders obtained are call converse Preorder, converse Postorder, converse inorder.

So

  • Converse Inorder: Right then Root then Left
  • Converse Preorder: Root then Right then Left
  • Converse Postorder: Right then Left then Root
